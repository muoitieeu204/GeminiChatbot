## Key Concepts

### ?? Question Relevance Filtering (Like NotebookLM)

```
User asks: "What's the weather today?"
    ?
    ?
CheckQuestionRelevanceAsync()
    ?
    ??? Send to Gemini:
    ?   "Is this question related to the document?"
    ?   Document preview: [first 1500 chars]
    ?   Question: "What's the weather today?"
    ?
    ??? Gemini responds: "NO"
        ?
        ??? Return error:
        "I can only answer questions related to the
            content of the uploaded PDF document."

User asks: "What is the main topic?"
    ?
    ?
CheckQuestionRelevanceAsync()
    ?
    ??? Send to Gemini:
    ?   "Is this question related to the document?"
    ?   Document preview: [first 1500 chars]
    ?   Question: "What is the main topic?"
    ?
    ??? Gemini responds: "YES"
    ?
        ??? Continue to full analysis ?
```
